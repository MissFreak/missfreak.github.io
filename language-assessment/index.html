COURSE TITLE: Language Testing and Assessment

INSTRUCTOR: Xiaoying Wang EMAIL: xywang@bfsu.edu.cn

STUDENTS: 2nd Year

TIME: 10:10-12:00 (Friday) PLACE： Room 307, SEIS Building

OFFICE: Room 506, SEIS Building OFFICE HOURS: 1:00-3:00 pm, Friday

syllabus
I. Course objectives: This course will introduce students to the why, what, and how of language testing and assessment, specifically, why there are various types of language tests and assessments, what language testers/ teachers usually test/assess, what is usually involved in designing and developing a test/ assessment for a particular purpose, how the four language skills are often tested/ assessed, and what are the features of a high-quality language test/ assessment. Moreover, ways to determine the quality of a language test/assessment will be discussed in order to arouse students’ interest in doing research in this field.
